About Me

I'm a Ph.D student advised by Sungsoo Ahn (@ KAIST 09.2025~). My research goal is to build generative models for solving real-world problems that require diverse candidate solutions, e.g., drug discovery. I especially focus on various types of generative models, including large language models (LLMs) [P1,C4], Generative Flow Networks (GFlowNets) [C2,C3], and diffusion probabilistic models (DPMs) [C1] for inferring diverse solutions. I am also interested in machine learning for medical and biological domains [J1,J2,C2,C3].
