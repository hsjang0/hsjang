About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student advised by Sungsoo Ahn at KAIST (2025.09~). My research focuses on drug discovery, with recent work on foundation models for molecular learning [P2] and prior work on generative modeling approaches including Large Language Models (LLMs) [P1,C4], Generative Flow Networks (GFlowNets) [C2,C3], Diffusion Probabilistic Models (DPMs) [C1], and Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) [J2]. I am also interested in machine learning for medical domains [J1].
